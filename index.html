The Library of Alexandria

Professional ‧ Noble ‧ Discrete ‧ Dependable

The Library of Alexandria is a superhero intelligence agency in Alexandria, Egypt responsible for solving problems in the world that ordinary citizens can’t accomplish. Despite the importance of their work, they are a small organization of roughly two hundred employees, called Scholars, who are then assembled into teams and sent across the world to do stealth missions for those who hire them. Led by Oshira Buhari, she oversees formation of teams and mission assignments to ensure the best teams for each mission. The Library provides complete anonymity for its clients and Scholars, and provides priceless training for superheroes and intelligence operations. It is also a suitable place for familial units and team building.


Think you may have a power? Look into our three types of Chi, the three subsets of powers. Can you lift cars? You might have Chi du Soma, which affects the body and the feats it can do. Can you move things with your mind? You might have Chi du Nous, which allows you to use the full potential of your mind to influence the world and others. Or perhaps you have the power to heal others with only a touch. You may have Chi du Spiritus, a secretive power that affects the soul and how it can influence other people's souls. Whatever power you have, there is always a place at the Library for you.

Meet the Team


Oshira Buhari (she/her/hers) · Vocal Projection · Director

Oshira oversees all functionality of the Library, including team formation and missions. Along with her husband and daughter, Oshira has served the Library for nearly 20 years and has offered quality and decisive leadership.


Zaki Buhari (he/him/his) · Shapeshifting · Director

Zaki has served as the Director of the Board alongside his wife for nearly 20 years and in charge of internal development of the Scholars. Along with supplying scholarships for university and apprenticeships, Zaki also oversees internal schooling and events for Youth Scholars to enrich their development.


Avery Marconi (he/him/his) · Telepathy · Head of Security

Avery has kept the Library safe from outside invaders for nearly 10 years and regularly performs internal screenings to ensure transparency and accountability inside the Library.


Philomena Buhari (she/her/hers) · Invisibility · Youth Scholar Liason

Philomena oversees the growth and development of the Youth Scholars, and works alongside her father to ensure that Youth Scholar education is robust and enriching. Philomena is the youngest Youth Liason ever, gaining the position at thirteen years old.
